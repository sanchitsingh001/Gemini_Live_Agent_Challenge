On a fog-drenched evening, a stranger arrives in the isolated town of Black Hollow. The streets are empty, doors are barred, and candles burn late behind shuttered windows. Everywhere they go, people whisper about a ghostly figure that appears at the edge of town each night, staring silently toward the crumbling church on the hill. As the stranger searches for answers, they uncover fragments of a long-buried secret binding the town to the restless spirit—one that can only be resolved before the next moonrise, or Black Hollow will be lost to the darkness forever.
